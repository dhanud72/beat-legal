Privacy Policy

Beat Music and MP3 Player · Last updated: 17 June 2026

Beat Music and MP3 Player collects no personal data. It has no account or login, shows no ads, contains no analytics or tracking, and sends nothing to any server. Everything stays on your device.

1. The short version

Beat is a fully offline music player. It reads the audio files already on your device so it can play them, and it saves your playlists, favorites and settings locally. That is all. There are no servers involved, so there is nothing for us to see, store, sell, or share.

2. Information we do not collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account information.
  • No location data.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No usage analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.
  • No contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to playing your music.

3. Permissions and why they are used

The app requests only the permissions it needs to play your music, and uses them entirely on your device:

  • Music and audio / storage access — to find and play the audio files saved on your device.
  • Notifications — to show playback controls (play, pause, skip) in the notification shade and on the lock screen.
  • All files access — requested only if you choose to delete a track, edit its tags or cover art, or create a backup file. It is used solely on your device and only when you trigger one of those actions.
  • Ignore battery optimizations — optional, to keep playback from being killed by the system during background play.

4. Where your data is stored

Your playlists, favorites, play counts and app settings are stored locally on your device only. If you create a backup, it is a small file saved to your own storage (and shared only if and where you choose to send it). None of this is transmitted to us or any third party.

5. No ads, no third parties

This version of the app contains no advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics or crash-reporting services. It does not request internet access.

6. Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

If the app changes in a way that affects this policy (for example, if optional ads were ever added in the future), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change.
